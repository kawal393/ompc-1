### Attested Statement (Verbatim)
“Accelerated computing and generative AI are inflection points that are driving the next era of computing.”

### Source Reference
NVIDIA FY2025 Annual Report, Letter to Shareholders

### Attestation Purpose
This attestation records NVIDIA’s public declaration that accelerated computing and generative AI represent a fundamental inflection in the global computing paradigm.

### APEX Interpretation
NVIDIA is asserting control over the **execution layer of intelligence**. By framing accelerated computing as the next era, NVIDIA positions its hardware, software stack, and developer ecosystem as the compulsory substrate through which modern AI systems are trained, deployed, and scaled.
