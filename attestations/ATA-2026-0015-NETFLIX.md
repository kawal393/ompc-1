### Attestation Purpose
This attestation records Netflix’s public declaration that data and machine learning are core decision-making systems governing content production, personalization, and global distribution.

### APEX Interpretation
Netflix is operating as **narrative infrastructure**. By embedding machine learning across creation, recommendation, and delivery, Netflix algorithmically shapes global attention, culture, and perception at scale. Control of narrative flow becomes a form of soft power exercised continuously through data-driven optimization.
