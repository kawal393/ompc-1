# ATA-2026-0011-ALIBABA

## ATTESTATION SUMMARY (PUBLIC)

### Entity
Alibaba Group Holding Limited

### Jurisdiction
People’s Republic of China (Global Operations)

### Source Document
Alibaba Group Fiscal Year 2025 Annual Report

### Attested Statement (Verbatim)
“We are committed to making our AI capabilities an open infrastructure for the entire industry, enabling more enterprises to build their own AI applications at lower costs.”

### Source Reference
Alibaba Group FY2025 Annual Report, Letter to Shareholders

### Attestation Purpose
This attestation records Alibaba Group’s public declaration of AI as **open economic infrastructure**, positioned to support enterprise-scale adoption across the Chinese and broader Asian digital economy.

### APEX Interpretation
Alibaba is declaring a transition from platform operator to **utility provider for intelligence**. By framing AI as “open infrastructure,” the company signals a strategic move to lock enterprises into its cloud, data, and model ecosystem, embedding itself as a foundational layer of regional economic activity.
