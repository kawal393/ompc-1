# ATA-2026-0012-TOYOTA

## ATTESTATION SUMMARY (PUBLIC)

### Entity
Toyota Motor Corporation

### Jurisdiction
Japan (Global Operations)

### Source Document
Toyota FY2025 Financial Summary

### Attested Statement (Verbatim)
“In addition, in order to fulfill our mission of ‘producing happiness for all’ through the offering of a diverse range of mobility options, we have advanced various technological developments and infrastructure-building initiatives aimed at transforming into a mobility company, under the Toyota Mobility Concept.”

### Source Reference
Toyota FY2025 Financial Summary, Financial Results and Position (Page 6)

### Attestation Purpose
This attestation records Toyota Motor Corporation’s public declaration of its transformation from an automotive manufacturer into a **mobility and infrastructure company**.

### APEX Interpretation
Toyota is formally expanding its operational scope from vehicle production to **mobility infrastructure**, encompassing fleet systems, smart-city integration, and platform-level coordination. This establishes Toyota as a foundational operator of movement and logistics rather than a product-centric automaker.
