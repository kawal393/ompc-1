# ATA-2026-0013-TSMC

## ATTESTATION SUMMARY (PUBLIC)

### Entity
Taiwan Semiconductor Manufacturing Company (TSMC)

### Jurisdiction
Taiwan (Global Operations)

### Source Document
TSMC 2024 Annual Report

### Attested Statement (Verbatim)
“Continued AI-related demand in 2025 supports our already-strong conviction that the structural demand for energy-efficient computing will accelerate, as everything around us becomes more intelligent and connected.”

### Source Reference
TSMC 2024 Annual Report, Letter to Shareholders (Outlook)

### Attestation Purpose
This attestation records TSMC’s public declaration that demand for AI is a **structural shift** centered on energy-efficient computing, not a temporary cycle.

### APEX Interpretation
TSMC is positioning itself as the **gatekeeper of the global power-to-intelligence ratio**, where process technology and advanced packaging define the ceiling of AI deployment worldwide. Control over energy-efficient silicon becomes control over AI scale.
