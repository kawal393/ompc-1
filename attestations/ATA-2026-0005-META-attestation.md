## Attestation Scope

This attestation records **verbatim, publicly disclosed statements** made by Meta Platforms, Inc. concerning its AI systems, data infrastructure, governance posture, and operational risks.

All statements below are **direct extracts or faithful factual representations** from publicly released documents.

No interpretation, inference, or extrapolation is applied.

---

## Attested Statements (Frozen)

### Statement 1 — AI as Core Business Infrastructure

Meta Platforms, Inc. publicly states that artificial intelligence is fundamental to its products, services, and long-term strategy, including content ranking, advertising systems, safety tooling, and product development.

**Source:** Meta Platforms, Inc. Annual Report (Form 10-K)

---

### Statement 2 — Large-Scale Model Development

Meta confirms the development and deployment of large-scale AI models, including foundation models, and discloses that such models require substantial compute, data, and energy resources.

**Source:** Meta AI public research disclosures and sustainability reporting

---

### Statement 3 — Open Model Strategy

Meta publicly states its intent to release certain AI models under open or open-weight licenses while retaining control over training data, infrastructure, and deployment contexts.

**Source:** Meta AI official publications and policy statements

---

### Statement 4 — Data Center Expansion

Meta discloses ongoing expansion of global data center infrastructure to support AI training and inference workloads, explicitly linking infrastructure investment to AI capability growth.

**Source:** Meta Platforms, Inc. Sustainability Report 2025

---

### Statement 5 — Governance and Risk Acknowledgement

Meta acknowledges material risks related to:
- AI misuse
- Algorithmic amplification
- Regulatory intervention
- Data protection and privacy compliance

Meta states that failure to manage these risks could materially and adversely affect its business.

**Source:** Meta Platforms, Inc. Form 10-K — Risk Factors
