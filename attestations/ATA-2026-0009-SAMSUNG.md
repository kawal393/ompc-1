# ATA-2026-0009-SAMSUNG

## ATTESTATION SUMMARY (PUBLIC)

### Entity
Samsung Electronics Co., Ltd.

### Jurisdiction
Republic of Korea (Global Operations)

### Attested Statement (Verbatim)
“We are accelerating the integration of artificial intelligence across our semiconductor, device, and platform businesses to enhance performance, energy efficiency, and user experience.”

### Source
Samsung Electronics Sustainability Report 2025

### Attestation Purpose
This attestation records Samsung Electronics’ public declaration of AI integration as a core operational strategy spanning semiconductors, consumer devices, and platform infrastructure.

### APEX Interpretation
Samsung is formally declaring AI as a **horizontal control layer** across silicon manufacturing, consumer hardware, and software ecosystems. This positions Samsung not merely as a device manufacturer, but as a vertically integrated **AI infrastructure conglomerate** controlling fabrication, deployment, and end-user interfaces.

### Status
ATTESTED — FIXED RECORD
