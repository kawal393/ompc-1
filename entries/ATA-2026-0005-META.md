## Primary Business Functions

Meta Platforms, Inc. operates global-scale digital platforms and AI systems, including but not limited to:

- Social networking and communication platforms (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp, Messenger)
- Digital advertising infrastructure
- AI model development and deployment
- Large-scale data center and compute infrastructure
- Open-source AI model publication and research

---

## AI and Data Infrastructure Role

Meta publicly positions itself as:

- A developer of **large-scale foundation models**, including open-weight AI systems
- An operator of **planetary-scale data ingestion and behavioral modeling systems**
- A publisher of **open AI research** intended to influence global AI development norms

Meta’s AI strategy emphasizes openness in model weights while retaining proprietary control over:
- Training data sources
- Deployment infrastructure
- Platform-level integration and feedback loops

---

## Governance and Risk Statements (Publicly Disclosed)

Public disclosures indicate that Meta acknowledges material risks related to:

- AI safety and misuse
- Algorithmic amplification effects
- Data governance and privacy compliance
- Regulatory intervention across multiple jurisdictions
- Content moderation at scale

Meta reports ongoing investments in internal governance frameworks, oversight boards, and AI risk mitigation processes, as described in its sustainability and annual filings.

---

## Infrastructure Footprint

Meta operates and expands:

- Hyperscale data centers across North America, Europe, and Asia
- Custom AI compute stacks (including accelerated hardware)
- Long-term energy procurement agreements to support AI growth

Infrastructure investments are explicitly linked to AI model training, inference, and platform optimization.
