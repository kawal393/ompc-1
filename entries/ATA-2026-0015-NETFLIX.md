## 2. THE CLAIM (VERBATIM)

* **Source:** Page 8, FY 2025 Form 10-K

## 3. THE APEX DECOMPRESSION
Netflix is signaling that the era of "Passive Streaming" is under threat. [cite_start]By explicitly naming **Generative AI** as a rapidly evolving risk, they admit that the barrier to entry for professional-grade content is collapsing[cite: 593, 594]. [cite_start]Netflix's defensive strategy is to pivot from being a "Library" to being a **"Live Experience & Gaming Platform"**[cite: 571, 574, 615]. [cite_start]They are moving toward a model where they don't just host video, but own the **Attention Infrastructure** of the household[cite: 493, 499, 501].

## 4. THE PREDICTION
* **Timeline:** 2026-2028
* **Outcome:** We predict a "Content Dilution" event. As Gen-AI allows competitors to create high-fidelity content at near-zero cost, Netflix will be forced to integrate AI-driven personalization that generates "synthetic scenes" in real-time to maintain subscriber stickiness. Expect the first "AI-Co-Produced" original series by Q4 2026.
