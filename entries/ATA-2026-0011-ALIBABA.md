## Public Statement (Recorded)

Alibaba Group Fiscal Year 2025 Annual Report

---

## Structural Verdict (APEX)

This statement signals a strategic transition from consumer-facing commerce to **intelligence-as-utility**.

Key structural elements:
- AI framed as *infrastructure*, not product
- Cost compression positioned as ecosystem expansion
- Small and medium enterprises targeted as dependency anchors

**Interpretation:**  
Alibaba is positioning its cloud and model stack (Alibaba Cloud / Tongyi) as the default intelligence layer for regional commerce, manufacturing, and logistics networks.
