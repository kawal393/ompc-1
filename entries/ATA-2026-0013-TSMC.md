## 3. THE APEX DECOMPRESSION
TSMC is framing AI demand as a multi-year structural shift, not a cycle. The phrase “structural demand for energy-efficient computing” is a declaration that the true bottleneck is not “AI models,” but the **power-to-compute ratio**. This positions TSMC as the gatekeeper of the world’s energy-efficient intelligence throughput: the entity whose process, packaging, and capacity decisions determine the ceiling of global AI deployment.

## 4. THE PREDICTION
* **Timeline:** 2025–2027
* **Outcome:** We predict “energy-efficient compute” becomes the dominant procurement constraint across AI buildouts, accelerating demand concentration into the foundry + advanced packaging stack. This will intensify geopolitical and industrial leverage around leading-edge capacity decisions and packaging throughput.
